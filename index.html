Simple Setup

Get Started in 3 Steps

Setting up Mirage Notify is quick and easy. Start protecting your privacy in minutes.

1

Install & Grant Permission

Download Mirage Notify from the link below and grant notification access permission. This allows the app to read and modify your notifications.

2

Create Your Rules

Set up custom rules for specific apps. Choose what to modify - titles, content, or sender names. Use find-and-replace, masking, or complete renaming.

3

Enjoy Your Privacy

That's it! Mirage Notify works in the background, automatically modifying notifications based on your rules. Check the logs anytime to see what's been modified.
